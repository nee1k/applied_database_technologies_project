About the Big Cities Health Coalition

Urban health has great potential when using the wealth of data in the Big Cities Health Inventory (BCHI). The entire potential of BCHI data can help 35 important U.S. towns because of the CityHealth Explorer platform. The capacity of CityHealth Explorer to quickly standardize and compare measurements across cities is one of its core strengths. Apart from guaranteeing consistency, this standardization facilitates comprehensive analysis and benchmarking endeavors. With over 150,000 data points encompassing over 120 health and demographic categories, CityHealth Explorer offers a thorough picture of urban health dynamics.
